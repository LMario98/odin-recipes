Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Oil: This traditional Italian recipe starts with olive oil.
  • Guanciale: Buy guanciale (or cured pork cheek) at your local butcher. If you canâ€™t find it, you can substitute unsmoked bacon or pancetta.
  • Spaghetti: Use store-bought or homemade spaghetti noodles. If you like, you can substitute bucatini for the spaghetti.
  • Eggs: Three large eggs add richness and flavor.
  • Cheese: Opt for hand-shredded Pecorino Romano cheese. You can substitute Parmesan, if preferred.
  • Seasonings: This flavorful spaghetti alla carbonara is simply seasoned with salt and pepper.

instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cheese, and pepper. Combine with the hot pasta, adding reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  4. Mix in the guanciale and serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
