Pasta alla Norma

Ingredients

  • Eggplant: 1 large, cut into cubes
  • Olive oil: for frying
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes: 1 can (28 oz)
  • Fresh basil: a handful, torn
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
  • Pasta: 400g (penne or rigatoni)
  • Ricotta cheese: for serving

Instructions

  1. Fry the eggplant in olive oil until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saut√© garlic, then add crushed tomatoes and basil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and combine with the sauce.
  4. Serve topped with fried eggplant and a dollop of ricotta cheese.
