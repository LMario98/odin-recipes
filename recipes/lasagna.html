Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that you can try at home!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
